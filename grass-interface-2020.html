Grass interface

Grass Interface confronts us with the end of the world, when the last patch of grass requires attention and care. The project invites us to explore grass as a sensual interface, translating the interaction between human and plant into a data visualization that retains the fluidity of the original touch and asks us to become aware of the ways in which we may connect with a natural medium that is often taken for granted.
